Wiltshire, England
1749

Rural England is in the grip of change and growing discontent as individual ownership replaces common rights to land.

When a local man is found dead near the village of Bradley, it appears to be a simple case of a poacher killed by a gamekeeper’s deadly trap. Yet all is not what it seems—at least not to Elias, the dead man’s ten-year-old son.

In this strategy card game, you are Elias. To uncover the truth, you must investigate the mystery, heed the warnings of the wild, and navigate dangerous situations, all while avoiding the watchful eyes of the local elite.
